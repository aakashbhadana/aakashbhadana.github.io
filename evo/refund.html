Refund Policy

Returning an opened Caeli Mask

You are eligible to return or request a replacement of your opened Caeli mask if the following conditions are met:

  • Your order has a defect which prevents your mask from functioning correctly and is deemed to be caused during the manufacturing or shipping process. Clear photo evidence of this defect is required.
  • Your product packaging is significantly damaged upon arrival.
  • You must make your request within 30 days of your full payment being made for your Caeli Evo mask.
  • Items sent back to us without first requesting a return will not be accepted.

Returning your Caeli Evo mask when not purchased through our website:

  • If you purchased through another website or through a retail store, please contact this party for your refund request.

Terms and conditions:

  • Shipping charges paid by the customer cannot be refunded.
  • All refunds will be made in Indian Rupees and any currency fluctuations will not be refunded.
  • A 2% refund charge will be deducted from the order amount when a refund is provided due to any reason.
  • The product packaging is ‘significantly damaged’ if the box has been crushed or penetrated. Minor dents or imperfections do not constitute ‘significant damage’ and are reasonably assumed to occur during shipping and handling.
  • If the clear plastic wrapping, which surrounds the Caeli Mask box packaging, is broken, opened, damaged or tampered with, the product is deemed to be ‘used and opened’.
  • Items sent back to us without first requesting a return will not be accepted.
