TECH

ReWrite | Digital Notebook

Active : May 2017 to Nov 2017

" Replace Traditional Paper "

Overview

Education sector is growing at a pace like never before, with it the consumption of paper in schools and institutes has become a matter of concern. There is an advancement of technology in recent years but education remains the only field which is yet to be digitized.

ReWrite is a digital notebook that replaces your stack of stationary and overcomes that limitations of traditional paper. ReWrite is a perfect tool for the students, artists, professionals, journalists, writers and everyone who's life revolves around paper.

ReWrite helps students to take handwritten notes and share among each other easily in contrast to traditional paper that requires storing, carrying, and high cost for purchasing notebooks, books, thus reducing the heavy use of paper and overcoming the problems posed by traditional paper.

Sneak Peek

ReWrite includes features like inbuilt E-book store, E-mail manager, Language translator, device casting and pronunciation assist to make working on it easy and fun
