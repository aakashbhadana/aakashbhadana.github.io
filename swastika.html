Swastika

Swastika is an online platform that allows two-way interaction between Donors and NGOs, it provides a solution to those who want to donate but have difficulty in finding appropriate and needful NGO nearby and plus it also features an exclusive portal for NGOs that allows them to source donations in the form of funds or resources from targeted audience.

Swastika also includes an inbuilt discussion forum that allows both NGOâ€™s and donors to share thoughts and questions with everyone. It can be used as a tool to gather participants and volunteers for an event. There is also a special feature for broadcasting urgent help and request messages thus adding social integrity to the platform.

Sneak Peek

Swastika will give virtual coins for every donation or social activities that the user participate in, these coins can then later be redeemed by the users at our partnered brands
